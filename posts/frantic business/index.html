Frantic Business

Fri 23 March 2018

Most of us are involved in some kind of frantic business, but what I want to suggest to you is that this frantic business is your yoga, your way of liberation. You don't have to get out of it, you don't necessarily have to drop out. Although it is important for some people. It depends whether you have the vocation to drop out or the vocation to stay in. I would not only say it would help to make your everyday life into a yoga but you won't really be able to stand it if you don't. It is not as some people say: can we Westerners practice oriental disciplines; the question is if you can't, you are not going to survive.

Alan Watts
