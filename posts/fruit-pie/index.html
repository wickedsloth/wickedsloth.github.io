Fruit Pie

Mon 05 March 2018

This is a ultra simple, minimalist and fat free fruit pie recipe.

Ingredients:

  • 3 eggs
  • 50 g sugar
  • 150 g flour
  • Fruit (2 sliced apples or blueberries for eg.)

Preparation:

  1. Separate egg whites and yolk.
  2. Mix the yolk with the sugar and flour. Add enough water to allow mixing.
  3. Gently fold the yolk/flour mix into the egg white.
  4. Beat the white eggs into fluffy white cloud.
  5. Put the pie in the oven for 40-60 minutes depending on the types of fruit (45 min for apples, 60 minutes for blueberries)

Enjoy!
