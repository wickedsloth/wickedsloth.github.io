Posture Defects

Mon 05 March 2018

Modern life is hell for posture. Having to hunch over a desk for 10 hours a day for 45 years is not how we were designed to navigate the world. Many people have the issues below:

  • Winged scapula
  • Rounded shoulders
  • Posterior or anterior pelvic tilt

Exercises that can make everything better:

  • Take rest every 30-45 minutes. Don't torture your body with hours of holding pattern
  • Stretch: Hamstring and abdominal muscles (yoga cobra pose is excellent), pec minor (doorframe). Do 3x 30 seconds for each.
  • Revitalize: Hip hinge, see here: 3 sets of 10
  • Push up plus to exercise the serratus anterior: 3 sets of 10. If too hard, do the exercise on the knees rather than the full fledged version.
